Deploying without automated testing, version control checks, and consistent builds introduces bugs and makes rollbacks difficult.

Automate testing, linting, and builds in your CI pipeline. Version every artifact. Make deployments repeatable and reversible. Clear deployment logs and monitoring catch issues early. Good CI/CD gives confidence that code reaching production has been validated.
